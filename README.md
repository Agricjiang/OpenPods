# OpenPods
Making detergent pod technology transparent, accessible, and verifiable for everyone.
OpenPods

An Open-Source Laundry Pod Formulation Project Led by Unimasses

Mission

OpenPods is an open-source initiative led by Unimasses to build a transparent, verified, and continuously evolving knowledge base for laundry pod formulations.

The project aims to provide publicly accessible formulation data, laboratory validation results, manufacturing guidance, and performance benchmarks for water-soluble detergent pods. By making foundational formulations and experimental data openly available, OpenPods helps shorten product development cycles, reduce technical barriers, and accelerate innovation across the detergent industry.

Why OpenPods?

Today, much of the detergent pod industry operates as a "black box." Critical information regarding formulation design, ingredient functions, performance testing, and manufacturing optimization is often proprietary and inaccessible to new entrants.

This lack of transparency creates several challenges:

Long and expensive development cycles
Repeated trial-and-error across companies
High barriers for startups and regional manufacturers
Limited consumer understanding of product composition
Difficulty comparing performance claims across brands

OpenPods seeks to change this.

Our Vision

We believe that fundamental detergent technology should be more open, transparent, and accessible.

By creating a community-driven repository of validated formulations and experimental results, we aim to:

Establish open reference formulations for laundry pods
Document ingredient functions and performance characteristics
Share laboratory testing methodologies and data
Promote transparency in formulation development
Lower development costs across the industry
Encourage collaboration between formulators, manufacturers, suppliers, and researchers
Improve consumer understanding of cleaning products
What We Share

The project may include:

Laundry pod formulations
Dishwasher pod formulations
Surfactant systems
Enzyme systems
Fragrance and preservation systems
PVA film compatibility studies
Stability testing data
Cleaning performance benchmarks
Manufacturing guidelines
Cost optimization models
Sustainability assessments
Open Science for Cleaning Products

OpenPods is not intended to replace commercial innovation.

Instead, it provides an open foundation upon which companies, researchers, and entrepreneurs can build better products faster.

We believe that shared knowledge benefits the entire ecosystem by reducing duplicated effort, encouraging scientific validation, and promoting greater transparency between manufacturers and consumers.

Led by Unimasses

As a company focused on detergent pod technologies, manufacturing systems, and formulation development, Unimasses is committed to supporting a more open and collaborative future for the cleaning products industry.

We invite formulators, chemists, suppliers, manufacturers, researchers, and consumers worldwide to contribute to the project.

Together, we can make detergent technology more transparent, accessible, and innovative.
